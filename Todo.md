### Battery 5 minutes
### Power Adapter 1 hour or Never
#### I set Never because I have mine hooked up to monitors and I put it to sleep if I'm going to be away for awhile, but I don't want it to go to sleep in the middle of a long build or process.
### Uncheck '''Slightly dim the display when on battery'''
#### I just plug it in when it needs juice
